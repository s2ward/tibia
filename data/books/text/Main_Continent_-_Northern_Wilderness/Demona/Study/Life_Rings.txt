Information about the life rings
Written by Doneos
We found out that many of the undead own a life ring. We researched into it and found out that the life rings obtain a kind of magic.
It seems that the ring has the ability to resuscitate dead persons/monsters.
We havent found out yet how to release and to control the magic of the life rings.
But the time will come when we will find out its secret.
And it could be the key to our major goal!
