The trials of Zarganash grinded down even the most pious souls. Yet some enlightened sages used the travel to this corrupting place to challenge their purity and hone their understanding of their soul. The most potent druids drew amazing new powers by using their own souls to magnify the power of certain spells. One of the results that is easier to learn is this powerful spell. By drawing upon the soulpower and dedication, it enables the druid to heal an ally with powerful surge of nature energy. Yet it takes some toll on the soul of the user, which needs a short time to recover from the stress.
