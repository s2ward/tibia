The rune Yro is formed of five lines in roughly the shape of a fish. It is associated with a victory and stability. Inverted, it is associated with betrayal and sin.
The rune Mem is formed of six lines in roughly the shape of a hearth. It is associated with light and preservation. Inverted, it is associated with sloth and discord.
The rune Phir is formed of six lines in roughly the shape of a cat. It is associated with mystery and creation. Inverted, it is associated with vice and sorrow.
